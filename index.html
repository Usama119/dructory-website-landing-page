Drugtory is a mobile and web app created in house by the SSingularity team. Drugtory aims to solve the inventory issues that medicals face on a daily basis. Drugtory will also assist you in storing data on a centralized data center with which you can access all your data on your fingertips.

SERVICES WE OFFER

Centralized Data

We have a centralized data management system which allows all stakeholders to access data on one system avoiding duplication and inconsistencies in the data.

Staff Management

Drugtory also allows you to assign specific rights to your staff who will be accessing data on the system. For example, floor staff will be able to access billing data while the inventory managers can look at the inventory data only.

Inventory Management

Drugtory has an easy to access inventory management system where the inventory is updated in real-time giving you insights into your stocks highlighting the products you need to refill.

Support

Drugtory has a support centre where you will be able to interact with our support team to find solutions to the problems you are facing with the app.

Customer Management

Drugtory has a dedicated customer management system where all the customer data - right from purchase to billing will be stored. You can access data for any customer at any given time. This will in turn assist you with marketing any special offers, discounts or sales that you are running at your store to all your customers in no time.

Inventory Management

Drugtory has an easy to access inventory management system where the inventory is updated in real-time giving you insights into your stocks highlighting the products you need to refill.

Expiry Management

Drugtory has an unique feature of Product Expiry Management with which the system will automatically reflect the products which have an early approaching expiration date. This will help you manage your inventory efficiently.

User Management

Drugtory has an efficient user management system with an easy to access UI, assistance right from login to extracting reports, etc. which we bring to the table for you. We also have videos in-built in the app which will help you with issues related to functionalities of the app.

Real Time Synchronization

Data is synchronized in real-time on the Drugtory application with which you can see updated data at all points of time.

Cloud Database

All data that we will be using on the Drugtory application will be stored on a cloud database and will not be shared with any third party.
